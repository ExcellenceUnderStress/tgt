# TurboGixxer V2 Brand Brief

## Core Brand Traits

- Respected
- Technical
- Proven
- Disciplined

## Brand Summary

TurboGixxer Tuning is an independent EFI calibration shop run by Kenny Sampson in Auburn, Washington. The brand should be positioned as a serious, technically credible tuning practice built on long-term experience, proven competition background, and a disciplined approach to calibration rather than hype or shortcuts.

## Primary Brand Impression

The site should make visitors feel that TurboGixxer is:

- established
- technically sharp
- trusted by serious customers
- disciplined in process
- focused on real-world results, not marketing noise

## Who The Brand Is

TurboGixxer Tuning is run by Kenny Sampson, who has more than 17 years of tuning experience. He started tuning in Florida, later became the primary tuner at Speed Factory Racing in Tacoma from 2016 through 2020, and now runs an independent shop focused on direct customer relationships.

The business serves:

- local customers through dyno tuning appointments
- nationwide customers through remote e-tuning

## Background And Credibility

Kenny's background includes EFI calibration work on competitive Honda drag builds at Speed Factory Racing, including:

- Sport Front Wheel Drive Civic program
- a front-wheel-drive quarter-mile record run of 7.47 seconds before the 2020 crash
- AWD Honda program with trap speeds in the 190 to 200 MPH range

This background matters because it supports the brand's authority in:

- high-power turbo applications
- anti-lag systems
- boost management
- iterative, data-driven calibration under demanding conditions

## Current Business Focus

Since going independent, the focus has shifted away from race-team-only work and toward direct relationships with:

- street car owners
- project build customers
- higher-power combinations
- customers across a wider range of platforms and goals

## Service Positioning

### Remote E-Tuning

Remote tuning should be presented as a serious, revision-based calibration process rather than a fast one-file transaction.

Important characteristics:

- conservative starting basemap
- usually 5 to 17 datalog revisions
- built around actual vehicle data
- best for customers willing to communicate and log properly

### Dyno Tuning

Dyno sessions should be presented as an in-person calibration path for local or travel-in customers who want real-time refinement and immediate feedback.

Important characteristics:

- by appointment
- useful for complex setups
- useful for diagnosis and fine-tuning

## Brand Philosophy

TurboGixxer should be described as methodical and conservative, with emphasis on:

- safe, reliable power
- drivability
- clean throttle response
- predictable power delivery
- iterative refinement
- real understanding of the vehicle

## Tuning Style

Key themes from the source material:

- Tunes start conservative
- Refinement happens through datalog analysis
- Real-world behavior matters
- Closed-loop fueling and safeguards matter where applicable
- Communication and explanation are part of the experience

## Best-Fit Customer

TurboGixxer is best for customers who:

- want a careful, data-based tuning approach
- value reliability and drivability alongside power
- are willing to invest time in the process
- want to understand what is being done to the vehicle

## Not-Ideal Customer

TurboGixxer is likely a weaker fit for customers who:

- want a tune finished instantly with minimal back-and-forth
- only care about the highest possible peak number
- want the cheapest option available

## Reputation And Social Proof

Known trust signals from the source material:

- 94 percent recommendation rate on Facebook
- nearly 100 reviews mentioned in source material
- recommendations on Honda-Tech
- recommendations on LS1Tech

Common praise themes:

- smooth e-tuning process
- responsive communication
- improved power
- improved drivability

Potential caution theme:

- some reviews mention communication delays

This should not be hidden, but the site should not center it either. It is better treated as an operational expectation to manage honestly.

## Tone Of Voice

The site copy should feel:

- direct
- confident
- clear
- technical without sounding academic
- grounded, not inflated
- mature, not trendy

The copy should avoid:

- exaggerated claims
- generic marketing filler
- flashy tuner clichés
- startup-style hype language

## Visual Direction

The site should visually communicate:

- technical maturity
- force under control
- competition-proven credibility
- premium restraint

The visual language should not feel:

- noisy
- juvenile
- overly trendy
- generic performance aftermarket
- overly corporate

## Motion Direction

Motion should feel:

- engineered
- controlled
- powerful
- disciplined

It should not feel:

- chaotic
- random
- showy without purpose

Recommended motion concept:

`Legacy Under Load`

Meaning:

- the brand has real history
- the work holds up under pressure
- the site should suggest force, telemetry, refinement, and control

## Primary Website Jobs

The website needs to:

- communicate who TurboGixxer is
- show why the brand is respected
- present services clearly
- show featured builds as proof
- support merch and Haltech shop visibility
- route visitors into contact and booking

## Conversion Priorities

Primary:

- Contact

Secondary:

- Browse curated merch and Haltech
