# TurboGixxer V2 Site Map

## Project Brief

TurboGixxer V2 is a cinematic, brand-first website that communicates legacy, recognition, featured builds, service paths, scheduling, and a curated merch plus Haltech shop. The primary conversion actions are contact and booking.

## Core Business Goals

- Establish TurboGixxer as a known and credible performance brand.
- Show proof through selected featured builds.
- Explain services clearly enough that visitors can choose the right path.
- Support qualified booking and project intake.
- Support a curated shop focused on merch and Haltech.

## Primary User Journeys

### New Visitor

1. Land on homepage.
2. Understand who TurboGixxer is.
3. Explore services or featured builds.
4. Submit contact or booking inquiry.

### Warm Lead

1. Land on homepage, services page, or contact page.
2. Confirm service fit.
3. Start booking or intake immediately.

### Existing Brand Follower

1. Land on homepage, builds page, or shop page.
2. Browse featured builds or curated products.
3. Reach contact or shop destination.

## Page Roles

### `/`

Purpose:
Brand statement, emotional entry point, routing into service, proof, shop, and booking.

Primary CTAs:
- Book
- Contact
- View Builds

### `/about`

Purpose:
Show history, credibility, philosophy, and who the customer is.

Primary CTAs:
- Contact
- Book

### `/pricing`

Purpose:
Present pricing for dyno, standalone, and remote tuning paths, with a build-type cheat sheet so visitors can self-select the right lane.

Primary CTAs:
- View Dyno
- View Remote
- Start Booking

### `/pricing/dyno`

Purpose:
Explain what dyno tuning includes, who it is for, prep requirements, and booking expectations.

Primary CTAs:
- Book Dyno
- Contact

### `/pricing/remote`

Purpose:
Explain remote tuning fit, platform requirements, process, and intake expectations.

Primary CTAs:
- Start Remote Intake
- Contact

### `/builds`

Purpose:
Show proof and credibility through curated featured builds.

Primary CTAs:
- Contact
- Book

### `/shop`

Purpose:
Route visitors into merch or Haltech and frame the store as curated, not bloated.

Primary CTAs:
- Shop Merch
- Shop Haltech

### `/shop/merch`

Purpose:
Present branded merchandise cleanly and simply.

Primary CTAs:
- Buy
- Contact

### `/shop/haltech`

Purpose:
Present a curated Haltech lane for customers who already know what they need or want guidance.

Primary CTAs:
- Inquire
- Contact

### `/contact`

Purpose:
Direct communication path for questions, project review, and general inquiries.

Primary CTAs:
- Submit Inquiry
- Go to Booking

### `/book`

Purpose:
Qualification-first booking flow for dyno, remote, or project review requests.

Primary CTAs:
- Submit Booking Request
