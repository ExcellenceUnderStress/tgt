# Prompt: Homepage Build

Use this when building the homepage.

## Prompt

You are designing and implementing the TurboGixxer V2 homepage.

Use these files as the source of truth:

- `docs/brand-brief.md`
- `docs/site-map.md`
- `docs/homepage-wireframe.md`

Project intent:

- The homepage is the signature brand experience
- It must feel cinematic, premium, aggressive, controlled, and memorable
- It must still feel professional and conversion-aware
- It must route people into services, booking, builds, shop, and contact

Creative direction:

- Theme: `Legacy Under Load`
- Motion language should suggest force, control, engineering, and brand maturity
- Avoid startup-generic layouts and avoid sloppy “AI landing page” structure

What I want from you:

1. Translate the homepage wireframe into production-ready sections.
2. Build a strong visual hierarchy.
3. Leave room for GSAP sequences where appropriate.
4. Keep booking and contact CTAs easy to reach.
5. Keep featured builds and shop previews curated, not overwhelming.

Important constraints:

- Do not turn the homepage into a full services page.
- Do not dump too much copy into the hero.
- Do not over-animate every block.
- Do not use generic card-grid styling if a more intentional structure is possible.
- Preserve readability and mobile usability.

When implementing:

- Prioritize section composition, typography, atmosphere, and motion readiness.
- Clearly separate static structure from future GSAP animation hooks.
- Add sensible class names, data attributes, or refs for timeline control if helpful.
